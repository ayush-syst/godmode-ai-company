## Outcome

Per [[../decisions/2026-06-13-first-product-gst-bill-assistant]], the engine's top *greenfield*
pick was **BillKaro** (GST invoicing for MSMEs). It was **not** chosen as the first product — the
founder's existing **GST Bill Assistant** is a stronger first move (mature, near-launch, better
wedge, real domain edge).

**BillKaro is retained here as a backlog / potential-feeder idea:** an MSME-facing invoicing
product whose invoices could later flow to CA firms using GST Bill Assistant (a natural two-sided
GST ecosystem). Notably, BillKaro's own build brief named "CA-partner referrals" as a key
distribution channel — which is precisely GST Bill Assistant's existing ICP.

## Why BillKaro lost the first-product slot (office-hours summary)

- MSME invoicing = a graveyard of funded incumbents (Vyapar, Khatabook, Zoho, Tally).
- Discretionary, price-sensitive willingness-to-pay.
- No founder edge vs funded incumbents — exactly the edge GST Bill Assistant provides.
